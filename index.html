My Favorite 1980s Movies

I love American films from the 80s! This is why I created this blog which is listing my Top 3 favorite movies from this decade.

Back to the Future
(1985)

Back to the Future is an American science fiction film directed by Robert Zemeckis

I love the whole Back to the Future trilogy for its humor and the classic 80s special effects. Even though the plot gets pretty crazy over time, the movie still manages to stick to its original well-known storyline involving the McFly family.

Top Gun
(1986)

Top Gun is an American action film directed by Tony Scott

The 2022 sequel of this film, Top Gun: Maverick, was one of the most popular movies this year and I love the 1986 movie just as much. There are a lot of special moments in this movie accompanied by an outstanding soundtrack.

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade
(1989)

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade is an American action-adventure film directed by Steven Spielberg

What can possibly go wrong when you have George Lucas as your co-writer? I love the acting by Harrison Ford and Sean Connery in this movie.
